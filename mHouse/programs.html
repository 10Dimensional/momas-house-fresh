Here are some of the things we look for in our volunteers

We make it our mission to work with you to provide a safe, rewarding experience for yourself and our residents. It is important for the success of our programs that our volunteers possess specific attributes.

Dedicated

Our residents have lived a life of chaos. A dedicated source of support and love is critical as they start on the right path.

Empathetic

Being able to put yourself in the shoes of our residents will help you provide the best support for each individual, based on their needs and circumstances.

Tough Love

It’s important to remember that our residents are survivors. To be successful, it will be important to provide firm, but loving guidance.

Stability

Your strong character will serve as an example to the residents of MOMA’s House. Your stability will empower them to make positive changes in their lives.

Advocate

As our residents go through their own program, they will need you in their corner, believing in them. Your faith will build their confidence and self-worth.

Disciplined

Just as our residents are expected to live by our program’s rules, we expect you to also respect the program and help guide residents down the right path.
