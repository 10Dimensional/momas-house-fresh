Some words from our MOMA

Domestic Violence and Human Sex Trafficking is a serious issue in Arizona

Domestic Violence

In Arizona, an arrest is made due to a physical assault case to a woman every 19 minutes and a domestic violence call every five minutes. With the prevelence of violence toward women, there is a great need for the services offered by MOMA's House directed at providing victimized women a safe, empowering environment.

Human Sex Trafficking

Phoenix, Arizona is unfortunately one of the hubs of human set trafficking in the United States. This is a major issue with 600,000 - 800,000 people being trafficked across boarders each year, but this is a crime that impacts individuals. Our work at MOMA's House helps victims of human sex trafficking find their identify and self-worth again.
